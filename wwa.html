Who We Are

A Worthy Cause

A worthy cause is a humanitarian organization that aims to make a positive impact on the world by uniting volunteers from all over the globe towards a common goal: to alleviate suffering and promote human welfare. Through our global network of volunteers, we strive to create a better future for people facing adversity, whether due to poverty, conflict, natural disasters, or other challenges. Our mission is to empower individuals to take action and make a difference in the lives of those in need, while fostering a culture of compassion and community. We believe that every person has the potential to contribute to positive change, and by working together, we can create a world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Nayr Oriuges

Nayr Oriuges has served as the President and CEO since 2016, and he brings not only a wealth of business experience (non-profit and corporate), but also a unique perspective to A WORTHY CAUSE. As a young child living in a toxic community in the Philippines during his early childhood, Nayr suffered a life-altering bout with Anxiety and Depression – a mental illness that greatly impacted his life and ostracized him from society, and ultimately, his family. But in God's grace Nayr overcome his mental health problems with the help of the experts.

When Nayr was only 5 years old, his father dropped him and his sister off an orphanage, where they spent the next 5 years before being adopted by a couple in the U.S. Nayr eventually graduated from Cornell University with a degree in Agricultural Economics and earned his MBA in Marketing and Finance from Northwestern University.

Prior to joining A Worthy Cause, Nayr held executive-level positions with nonprofits including Child Fund International, Heifer International, ChildHelp and WorldVision US and in corporations including: Bristol-Myers Squibb, Conagra Foods, AmeriTrade and Univera Life Sciences.

In 2016, Nayr was approached by A Worthy Cause. Because MAP is a Christian organization, Nayr saw this as an opportunity to live out his faith through his work. He has dedicated his life to bringing critically needed medicines and health supplies to some of the world’s poorest people, so that no child suffers the same fate that he did simply because of a lack of access to medicine.

God redeemed every experience in Nayr’s life, to prepare him for his role at A Worthy Cause. He is particularly motivated by Jesus’s words in Matthew 25:36, “I was sick, and you looked after me.” Nayr is passionate about A Worthy Cause mission and he invites you to join him in helping to provide life changing welfare to people in need around the world.
